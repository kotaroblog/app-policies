Privacy Policy

App: WorkoutDiary
Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

WorkoutDiary stores workout data entered by the user (exercise names, weights, reps, dates, and notes) only on your device. This data is never transmitted to external servers.

2. Advertising

This app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect information such as your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) and IP address for ad delivery purposes.

Personalized ads require your consent through the tracking permission dialog shown at app launch. If you decline, non-personalized ads will still be displayed.

For Google's privacy policy, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. In-App Purchases

This app offers subscriptions through Apple's StoreKit. Payment information is managed by Apple, and the developer does not have direct access to your credit card or payment details.

4. Data Storage

All workout data is stored in your device's local storage. Deleting the app will permanently remove all data.

5. Third-Party Sharing

This app does not share your personal information with third parties, except for information necessary for ad delivery.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as needed. Any changes will be communicated by updating this page.
