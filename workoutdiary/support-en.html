FAQ

How do I use the app?

Open the app and tap the "+" button to start a workout. Select an exercise, then enter the weight and reps to add a set. Use the template feature to quickly repeat your favorite routines with a single tap.

Is my data backed up?

Your data is stored locally on your device. If iCloud Backup is enabled on your iPhone, your workout data will be included in your device backup.

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → WorkoutDiary → Cancel Subscription. You can continue using Premium features until the end of your current billing period.

Will I be charged after the free trial?

Yes, your subscription will automatically begin after the 7-day free trial ends. You can cancel anytime during the trial period at no cost.

The app isn't working properly

Please try the following:
1. Force quit and relaunch the app
2. Restart your iPhone
3. Update to the latest version from the App Store
